Home/About Us

About Us

MyBodyGuide was created with one simple belief: everyone deserves clear, trustworthy nutrition guidance, in their own language, free of charge.

Our Mission

To make evidence-based nutrition knowledge accessible to everyone — regardless of background, budget, or location — through free calculators, an easy-to-search food database, and bite-sized educational content.

Our Vision

A world where understanding your own body and making informed food choices is simple, welcoming, and available in the language you think in.

Our Goals

We aim to constantly expand our halal food database, keep our nutrition facts aligned with recognized public health guidance, and grow our language coverage so more people can use MyBodyGuide comfortably.

Our Commitment

We are committed to a healthy-lifestyle-first approach: no fad diets, no miracle cures, and no unsafe calorie targets — just practical, science-informed tools and content.

Educational Purpose

MyBodyGuide is an educational platform. It does not replace personalized advice from a qualified doctor, dietitian, or other healthcare professional.
